Admission Process

Join Sitaram Inter College through our simple and transparent UP Board admission procedure

Apply Now View Fee Structure

Our UP Board Admission Journey

1

Inquiry & Registration

Begin by submitting an inquiry form online or visiting our campus. Our UP Board admission counselors will guide you through the process and help you register.

2

Submission of Documents

Submit required documents including previous academic records (marksheets), transfer certificate (if applicable), and identity proof as per UP Board requirements.

3

Interaction Session

Students and parents meet with our academic team to discuss educational goals and assess the student's readiness for our UP Board curriculum.

4

Admission Decision

Within 7 working days of document submission and interaction, you'll receive an admission decision via email and phone.

5

Fee Payment

Upon acceptance, complete the admission by paying the required UP Board fees within the stipulated time to secure your seat.

6

Orientation

Attend the orientation program before the academic session begins to familiarize yourself with school policies, faculty, and UP Board requirements.

UP Board Admission Dates

Regular Admissions

  • Application Opens January 15, 2025
  • Priority Deadline March 31, 2025
  • Final Deadline May 15, 2025

Late Admissions

  • Late Application Period May 16 - June 10, 2025
  • Seat Availability Basis Limited Seats
  • Academic Session Begins July 1, 2025

Note: Late applications may incur additional charges and are subject to seat availability. Early applications receive priority consideration for UP Board registration.

Required Documents for UP Board

Academic Records

  • Original marksheet of previous class
  • UP Board marksheet (for class 11/12)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school

Identity Proof

  • Birth Certificate (issued by Municipal Corporation)
  • Aadhaar Card (Student & Parents)
  • Passport size photos (4 copies, white background)

Application Forms

  • Completed UP Board admission form
  • Medical history form
  • Transportation request (if needed)

Financial Documents

  • Fee payment receipt (UP Board registration fee included)
  • Concession form (if applicable)
  • Scholarship documents (if applicable)
