Fee Related Questions

Fees are due according to the following schedule:

  • Annual Payment: Due by July 15th
  • Two-installment plan: 60% by July 15th and 40% by November 30th
  • Quarterly plan: Four equal payments due July 15, October 15, January 15, and April 15

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Online payments (credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI)
  • Bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS)
  • Cash payments at the school office
  • Cheques or demand drafts payable to "Sitaram Inter College"

Yes, late payments incur the following charges:

  • Up to 15 days late: ₹100 per day
  • 16-30 days late: ₹200 per day + temporary suspension of classes
  • Beyond 30 days: Admission may be cancelled with forfeiture of security deposit
