Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2002 by visionary educators, SRIC began as a small neighborhood school with just 280 students and 7 dedicated teachers. Our founders believed in creating an institution that would provide quality education accessible to all sections of society.

From our modest single-building campus, we have grown into one of Amroha's most respected senior secondary schools, serving over 1,200 students today while maintaining our commitment to academic excellence and character development.

Our Journey Through Time

Key milestones in the evolution of SRIC Senior Secondary

2002

The Founding Year

SRIC Educational Society established the school with 280 students and 7 teachers in a rented building. Our first batch of 5th standard students began their educational journey.

2005

First Own Campus

Moved to our permanent campus with 5 classrooms, an administrative block, and our first playground. Student strength grew to 450 students.

2010

Secondary Expansion

Added 8th-10th standards and became a full-fledged high school. Introduced our first science lab and library.

2013

Senior Secondary Status

Started 11th and 12th standard programs with Humanities stream. Built dedicated science labs and computer center.

2015

Science Stream Added

Expanded academic offerings by introducing Science stream for Classes 11 and 12, with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics.

2018

Academic Excellence

Achieved a remarkable milestone with a 98% pass rate in the Class 12 board examinations, reflecting our commitment to quality education.

2023

Today & Beyond

1,200+ students with 100% university placement assistance. Ranked among Amroha's top senior secondary schools with state-of-the-art facilities.

Our Campus Journey

Witness the remarkable transformation of our learning environment through the years

2002 - Humble Beginnings

Our original campus with just 3 classrooms, an office, and a shared courtyard that served as both playground and assembly area.

Foundation Year

"Where our educational journey began with 280 students and 7 dedicated teachers."

2008 - First Expansion

Added two new academic wings with 12 classrooms, our first science lab, and a small library. Established proper playground facilities.

Growth Phase

"Recognized as the fastest growing school in the district with 450 students."

2015 - Modernization

Constructed the multipurpose auditorium, computer lab, and sports complex with basketball, tennis and badminton courts.

Quality Upgrade

"Achieved senior secondary status and introduced our first smart classroom technology."

2023 - State-of-the-Art

Our current campus features smart classrooms, STEM innovation labs, digital library, solar panels, and eco-friendly infrastructure.

Future Ready

"Now educating over 1,200 students with 68 faculty members and counting."
