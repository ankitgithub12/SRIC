Our School's Journey

From humble beginnings to senior secondary excellence

2002 - Founding

Established as a primary school with 280 students and 7 teachers.

2005 - Secondary Expansion

Introduced classes 6th-8th, transforming the institution into a full-fledged high school.

2010 - Higher Secondary

Launched classes 9th-10th with a focus on Science, marking the beginning of the Higher Secondary section.

2013 – Introduction of Senior Secondary (Humanities)

Commenced senior secondary education by offering Classes 11 and 12 with a dedicated focus on Humanities, providing students with a strong foundation in arts and social sciences.

2015 – Expansion to Senior Secondary (Science Stream)

Expanded academic offerings by introducing the Science stream for Classes 11 and 12, equipping students with advanced knowledge in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.

2018 - Academic Excellence

Achieved a remarkable milestone with a 98% pass rate in the Class 12 board examinations, reflecting our unwavering commitment to quality education and student success

2020 - Solidified Institutional Standing

Building on the strong foundation established in 2010 and the proven academic excellence demonstrated in 2015, the institution solidified its reputation as a leading provider of quality Higher Secondary education.

Today - Premier Institution

Recognized as a leading educational institution, we offer a comprehensive and inclusive learning environment across all major streams—Humanities, Science, and Commerce. With a strong commitment to academic excellence, holistic development, and innovation, we continue to empower students to excel in academics and beyond.
