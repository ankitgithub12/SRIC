Our Curriculum Framework

SRIC follows the UP Board curriculum with enhancements to provide a holistic education that balances academic rigor with practical learning experiences.

Core Subjects

  • Hindi & English

    Comprehensive study of literature, grammar, and composition skills

  • Mathematics

    Algebra, Geometry, Statistics and problem-solving techniques

  • Science

    Physics, Chemistry, Biology with practical laboratory work

  • Social Science

    History, Geography, Political Science, and Economics

Additional Components

  • Computer Education

    Basic skills, programming fundamentals, and digital literacy

  • Physical Education

    Sports, yoga, and health education

  • Art Education

    Creative expression through visual and performing arts

  • Value Education

    Moral and ethical development programs

Science Stream (PCM)

  • Physics

    Theory and practical with modern laboratory equipment

  • Chemistry

    Inorganic, Organic and Physical Chemistry with experiments

  • Mathematics

    Calculus, Algebra, Vectors, and Probability

  • English

    Advanced literature and communication skills

Science Stream (PCB)

  • Biology

    Botany and Zoology with extensive laboratory work

  • Chemistry

    Inorganic, Organic and Physical Chemistry with experiments

  • Physics

    Theory and practical with modern laboratory equipment

  • English

    Advanced literature and communication skills

Core Subjects

  • History

    Indian and World History with focus on analytical skills

  • Political Science

    Indian Constitution, Political Theory and International Relations

  • Economics

    Micro and Macro Economics with Indian Economic Development

  • English

    Advanced literature and communication skills

Optional Subjects

  • Geography

    Physical and Human Geography with practical work

  • Psychology

    Human behavior and mental processes

  • Computer Science

    Programming and computer applications

  • Fine Arts

    Theory and practical in visual arts
